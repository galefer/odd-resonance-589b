Item #: SCP-L3W15

Object Class: Apollyon

Special Containment Procedures: Physical containment of SCP-L3W15 is currently impossible. Due to the entity's displacement capabilities, no cell, chamber, or dimensional anchor has proven effective. Current protocols focus on monitoring and appeasement.

Foundation web-crawlers (I/O-META) are to monitor global marriage registries and social media for sudden, unexplained guests at weddings. Upon confirmation of SCP-L3W15's location, MTF Theta-9 ("Party Crashers") is to be deployed with a standard Pacification Kit.

The Pacification Kit must contain:

  • One (1) porcelain mug.
  • Boiling water (approx. 100°C).
  • Yorkshire Tea bags (Brand Specificity is Mandatory).

Under no circumstances is generic tea or coffee to be offered to the entity. This will result in an immediate hostile state.

Description: SCP-L3W15 is a humanoid male entity possessing Type-Green reality-bending capabilities tied specifically to plumbing infrastructure. The entity has total access to a trans-dimensional subspace designated "The Shower Dimension."

Much like Class-V ectomorphs (e.g., "Bloody Mary") utilize reflective surfaces, SCP-L3W15 utilizes showers as intra-spatial gateways. The entity can enter any active shower unit and exit from any other shower unit globally, regardless of distance or water pressure.

Fig 2. The "Shower Dimension" captured via intra-dimensional lens.

Attempts to predict SCP-L3W15's movements have resulted in failure. The entity possesses a precognitive defense mechanism: if SCP-L3W15 becomes aware it is being tracked, or if it is "expected" to be in a specific location, it will instantaneously warp to a wedding ceremony currently occurring elsewhere on Earth.

It is unknown why the entity targets weddings, though it has been observed critiquing the catering before vanishing.

Addendum L3W15-A (Behavioral Note):
While generally chaotic, SCP-L3W15 displays extreme aggression if interrupted during transit. The only known method to cease hostility is the offering of tea. Note: Subject shows a molecular-level ability to distinguish between "Yorkshire Gold" and standard blends. Do not attempt to deceive SCP-L3W15.
